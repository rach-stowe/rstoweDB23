BUSINESS RULE SPECIFICATIONS

RULE INFORMATION

Statement:  This rule guarantees that no two customers have the same phone number. 

Constraint: When entering a phone number for a new customer, the phone number
            entered will be compared to the list of phone numbers stored in the Customer_Phones
            table to confirm that the number is unique before storing it in the customer_phone field.
